About This Certification

The ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Information Security Associate™ is an accredited professional certification that validates demonstrated knowledge and outstanding skills in information security management systems (ISMS). This certification is based on the internationally recognized ISO/IEC 27001:2022 standard, which provides a systematic approach to managing sensitive company information and ensuring its security.

This certification demonstrates competence in understanding and implementing information security controls, risk management processes, and compliance requirements that are essential for protecting organizational assets in today's digital landscape.

Key Skills & Knowledge

  • Information Security Management Systems (ISMS) framework and implementation
  • ISO/IEC 27001:2022 standard requirements and controls
  • Risk assessment and risk treatment methodologies
  • Security controls selection and implementation (Annex A controls)
  • Information security policies and procedures development
  • Compliance and audit processes for ISMS
  • Continual improvement and monitoring of security controls
  • Business continuity and incident management
