MCSI Remote Cybersecurity Internship

Intern • Remote • 2025

Overview

I completed a remote internship with MCSI focused on entry-level cybersecurity practices, network fundamentals, and hands-on labs that covered vulnerability assessment, log analysis, and basic incident response. The program combined guided labs, mentorship sessions, and a capstone lab where I applied what I learned to a simulated environment.

Key responsibilities & activities

  • Performed vulnerability scans and triaged findings using open-source tools.
  • Analyzed network traffic and logs to identify suspicious activity.
  • Worked through incident response playbooks and documented investigation steps.
  • Collaborated with mentors to design and implement remediation recommendations.

Projects & highlights

  • Capstone lab: Investigated a simulated intrusion, documented IOC, and produced a short incident report.
  • Completed a network mapping exercise and presented findings to the cohort.
  • Built a small checklist to standardize log collection and initial triage steps.

Skills & tools

Wireshark, Nmap, basic use of Metasploit framework for lab work, grep/awk for log parsing, and familiarity with common log formats (syslog, Windows Event logs).

Outcome & reflection

The internship accelerated my practical skills in identifying and documenting security issues in a lab environment. It improved my confidence with hands-on analysis, and reinforced the importance of clear documentation and reproducible investigation steps.

Timeline

Duration: 8 weeks — Spring 2025
